OUR SERVICES

HGV Medical

  • We provide cheap HGV medicals for HGV drivers nationwide.
  • Our doctor will conduct a full HGV medical which includes an eye test, and then fill out your D4 medical form. You will be advised on what to bring to your D4 medical.

    • PCV/Bus Medical

  • All bus, minibus and coach drivers require a PCV medical. We provide cheap PCV/Bus medicals nationwide.
  • The PCV medical is similar to the HGV medical and involves a full driver assessment and the completion of your D4 medical form.

    • Taxi Medical

  • We provide cheap taxi medicals and have been approved by a number of councils to provide taxi medicals.
  • Most councils accept medicals from all GMC registered doctors. Some councils require full medical records so please contact your council prior to booking your taxi medical

    • Business Services

  • We can provide driver medicals for your employees at a discount.
  • Our GMC registered doctor can conduct an HGV medical, Bus medical or Taxi medical, at your location. Contact us now for your bespoke driver medical package
